About Me
Bridging Language, Design & Technology
Welcome! I’m Anthony Wesonga, a multilingual interpreter, Swahili tutor, translator, and digital creative with a passion for empowering people and brands through language and design. I specialize in language interpretation, translation, web design, and digital graphics—offering a unique blend of human connection and modern technology to help individuals and organizations communicate with clarity, style, and purpose.

With over three years of remote interpreting experience and a growing portfolio in web and visual design, I serve clients across sectors such as healthcare, education, legal, nonprofit, and digital media—helping them cross not only linguistic boundaries but visual and technological ones as well.

My Expertise
🌍 Multilingual Interpretation & Translation
As a professional interpreter, I work fluently in English, Swahili, Luganda, Acholi, and Lugisu. I provide remote interpretation, sight translation, and document translation for medical consultations, legal hearings, educational meetings, business presentations, and social service settings.

I’ve worked with:

🧑🏾‍🏫 Swahili Language Tutoring
With over 3 years of tutoring experience, I offer customized Swahili lessons focused on conversation, grammar, culture, and confidence—ideal for both beginners and intermediate learners.

💻 Web Design
I design and build responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and modern UI practices—perfect for startups, small businesses, and personal portfolios. I focus on clean layouts, mobile optimization, and user-friendly interfaces to make your brand stand out online.

🎨 Still & Motion Graphics
From logo design to promotional banners and animated video intros, I create engaging visuals that elevate your digital identity. Whether it’s a static image for social media or a short animated clip for marketing, I bring clarity and creativity to your message.
