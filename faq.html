Got Questions?

What services do you offer?

We specialize in:
Remote Interpretation & Translation (Swahili, Luganda, Acholi, Lugisu, English)
Web Design using HTML and CSS (not WordPress)
Graphic & Motion Design (logos, posters, explainer videos, etc.)
Video Editing & Content Creation
Affiliate promotions and creative media strategy

Which languages do you interpret?

We interpret and translate fluently between:
English ⇄ Swahili
English ⇄ Luganda
English ⇄ Acholi
English ⇄ Lugisu

Do you work internationally?

Yes! All services are remote. We work with clients from the USA, Germany, Australia, El Salvador, and beyond.

How do I request a service?

Simply go to the Contact page or click on the Get Started button on the homepage. Fill out the inquiry form or reach out via email/WhatsApp.

What tools do you use?

We use industry-standard software such as:
Adobe Illustrator (Graphic Design)
Adobe Premiere Pro (Video Editing)
Adobe After Effects (Motion Graphics)
XTM (Translation)
Visual Studio Code (Website building)
Visit the Tools page for a complete list and descriptions.

How do you ensure quality?

We offer:
Certified interpretation with HIPAA and Medical Training
Clear communication throughout the process
Revisions where needed
On-time delivery
